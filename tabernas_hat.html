Introducing the ACIEN Blue Weaved Hat, TABERNAS, an embodiment of timeless elegance for your special occasions. Treasured for its escapist and oneiric design, this hat is meticulously woven using dry branches collected from the scenic mountains of southern Spain, employing ancestral techniques passed down through generations. With its atemporal appeal, the ACIEN Blue Weaved Hat is a captivating choice that will elevate your style and make a lasting impression on your special day.

  • Colour: Navy Blue (customisable)
  • Pineapple and Tencel blended organic yarn
  • Hand-dyed with plants
  • Weaved dry branches
  • Crochet flower straps (customisable)
  • Crochet simple stitch straps (customisable)
  • Upper hat hole for hair bun
  • Made in Spain

"It's more than just an accessory; it's a celebration of your roots, a symbol of support for regenerative agriculture, and a testament to the vital role of biodiversity in our very existence. Each hat makes a bold statement, while effortlessly becoming an essential component of every individual's resilient lifestyle. Embrace the passion and purpose woven into every dry branch, and let your hat reflect your commitment to a sustainable future"

Our regenerative plant based hats are made out of waste pineapple leaves yarns which are recyclable and biodegradable, saving up to 6kg of CO2 emissions from being released into the atmosphere per 1kg of yarn produced.

Our plant based yarns are dyed with care and delicacy with plants to protect the current biodiversity.

The esparto dry branches are purchased locally, supporting farmers of the south of Spain.
