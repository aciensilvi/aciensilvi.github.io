Our regenerative plant based hats are made out of nettle yarns from the Himalayan mountains which are recyclable and biodegradable.

Our plant based yarns are dyed with care and delicacy with invasive plants affecting the current biodiversity of London and China.

The esparto dry branches are purchased locally, supporting farmers of the south of Spain.
