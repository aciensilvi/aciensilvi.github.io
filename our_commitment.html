Our commitment

to you, the planet, and our biodiversity.







ACIEN is on a dedicated journey towards conscious luxury, striving to craft pieces that seamlessly combine allure and sustainability. Our commitment to regeneration is an ongoing evolution, acknowledging that perfection is elusive, yet every step we take holds significance. We aim to challenge conventions, unlock fresh possibilities, and drive transformative shifts within the fashion landscape.













Cruelty-Free Zone: No Leather, Fur, or Animal Testing

Vegetarianism is at the core of our values, so no animals are harmed by our products. Our collections steer clear of leather, fur, and exotic skins, and the glues in our products are completely free from animal ingredients. Our dedication to cruelty-free practices drives our values and pushes us to explore innovative materials.









Embracing Circularity

Our dedication to reducing waste propels us to embrace repurposed, recycled, and regenerative alternatives. We prioritise fully fashioning techniques, ensuring a process that generates no offcuts of yarns or fabric, maximising the utilisation of materials at every stage.

The "fully fashioning" technique in knitting involves shaping garment pieces directly while knitting, minimising the need for cutting and sewing. It aligns with zero waste principles by optimising material usage and reducing fabric waste. This approach allows for the creation of seamless, tailored garments.






Organically Certified Packaging and Paper

All our paper and packaging are organically certified using Organic GOTS or are derived from recycled sources. Our commitment aims to prevent net loss and mitigate contributions to deforestation.
